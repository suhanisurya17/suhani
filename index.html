Just Relax

Just Relax is a student-focused productivity program designed to enhance study sessions through meditation and immersive study ambiances. Built using Processing, it features Studyscapes, allowing users to select environments like a library, desk, or cafe, along with a Pomodoro timer for structured focus. The interface, designed in Figma, incorporates intuitive UI/UX elements such as a navbar and menu for seamless navigation.
